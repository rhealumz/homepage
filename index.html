Facts

Here's a fascinating fact about computer engineering: The first computer programmer in history was a woman named Ada Lovelace. She worked with Charles Babbage on his analytical engine in the mid-1800s and is credited with writing the first algorithm intended to be processed by a machine. Her contributions to the field laid the foundation for modern computer programming and established her as a pioneer in computer engineering. Ada Lovelace Day, celebrated annually on the second Tuesday of October, honors her achievements and aims to promote women in STEM fields..
